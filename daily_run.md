## Daily task

Find ONE current real-world decision scenario from today.

The topic must contain three layers:

### Layer 1 — Macro / Context Layer
Identify the external condition shaping the decision.

Examples:
- FOMC decision
- interest-rate path
- biotech catalyst
- earnings cycle
- policy shift
- market volatility
- regulatory change

### Layer 2 — Sector / Narrative Layer
Identify the specific sector, company, domain, or narrative affected.

Examples:
- Big Tech earnings
- biotech trial decision
- AI infrastructure spending
- startup product launch
- investment timing
- clinical strategy

### Layer 3 — Structural Tension Layer
Identify the actual decision conflict.

Examples:
- macro uncertainty vs earnings momentum
- clinical promise vs trial design weakness
- market opportunity vs execution capacity
- valuation upside vs liquidity risk
- legal argument strength vs enforceability risk
